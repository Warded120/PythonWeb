# Acme – Online Food Delivery Platform

## Overview
**Acme** is a modern food delivery service that combines ease of use with excellent customer experience.  
Users can register, browse meals, and place an order in just a few clicks.  
Orders are automatically processed, and customers can track delivery status in real time.

## Similar Platforms
- **Rozetka** – one of the largest Ukrainian online marketplaces offering delivery of groceries and household goods.
- **Sainsbury's** - one of the largest retail stores in Great Britain offering delivery of groceries and household goods.

## Key Features
- **Product Catalog** – searchable with filters (e.g., category, price, rating).  
- **User Dashboard** – manage profile and past orders.  
- **Cart & Checkout** – add meals, confirm, and pay.  
- **Delivery Options**  
  - For individuals: courier or pickup.  
  - For businesses: retail chains and warehouses.  
- **Payments** – pay online or with cash on delivery.

## Technical Requirements
- **Backend:** Python (Django) for core logic.  
- **Database:** PostgreSQL for storing users, products, and orders.  
- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS for the interface.  
- **Delivery Module:** Google Maps API for route tracking and address validation.
